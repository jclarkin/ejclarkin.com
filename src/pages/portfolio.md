### Item A - Essential Learning Skills

**Document:** [Destination Profile: *Becoming a Section Clarinetist with the NAC Orchestra* (PDF)](/portfolio/destination-profile-section-clarinetist.pdf) *(from Lesson 16)*

**Reflection - what this proves:** I chose to include my career research presentation because it shows my learning skills and how I can find and organize information about a job I'm interested in. It's also a good example of communication since I had to present that info so it made sense to other people. This kind of focus and being able to stick to a plan are employability skills that I use to get my work done.

### Item B - Teamwork Skills

**Document:** [Teamwork Self-Assessment & Improvement Plan](/skills-work-samples/) *(from Lesson 7 - see also the linked Skills & Work Samples page)*

**Reflection - what this proves:** The Lesson 7 self-assessment identified my primary teamwork roles as **Thinker and Carer** (with Leader as a backup), based on a 40-question questionnaire. The summative half of the task names three teamwork skills I am actively improving (*Use Numbers*, *Communicate*, *Work With Others*) and pairs each one with a concrete real-world practice. This document surfaces my teamwork skill in two ways: it shows I am self-aware enough to know my role on a team, and it shows I take ownership of growth instead of waiting for someone to point out gaps. In an orchestra, those are the qualities that keep a section playing as one voice.
